Description

Welcome to Professor Porky's Pizza Parlor, where the pizzas are ASS (Assembled Super Speedily)! Step into the shoes of a PPPP employee and bear witness to the chaos that goes on behind the scenes as you attempt to make as many pizzas as you can without messing up!

Prepare a pizza that matches the current order before the dough leaves the screen, but be careful not to make any mistakes or you'll have to start over. The conveyer belt will speed up and the orders will become more complex over time. Go for the highscore!

Click on the ingredient pile on the conveyor belt to get an ingredient, then drag it into your pizza. Toppings can't be removed, so make sure you're following the order exactly before placing a topping on the dough!
